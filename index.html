Three steps to magical fire

01

Light Your Fire

Get a good wood-burning fire going first — log burner, fire pit, open fireplace, campfire or BBQ. Any wood-burning fire works.

02

Place Unopened

Do not open the sachet. Place 1–3 whole sachets directly onto the burning wood. The whole sachet burns completely — nothing to clean up.

Watch & Enjoy

Within seconds the flames transform — greens, blues, purples and reds dancing for 20–45 minutes. Stand back and enjoy the show.
